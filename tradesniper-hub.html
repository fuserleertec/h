Frequently Asked Questions

What platforms does TradeSniper support?▾
TradeSniper currently offers indicators for ThinkorSwim (ThinkScript) and TradingView (Pine Script v5). The SMC Trade Journal App runs on web and mobile. We're actively working on additional platform support.
Is the Unified Smart Money Engine based on ICT concepts?▾
Yes — 100%. The USME is built entirely on Inner Circle Trader (ICT) and Smart Money Concept (SMC) methodology, including Order Blocks, Fair Value Gaps, BOS/CHoCH, SMT Divergence, Killzones, and multi-timeframe bias alignment.
Can I use these tools with a prop firm account?▾
Absolutely. Our indicators work on any standard ThinkorSwim or TradingView setup. Many of our users are funded prop traders using firms like Topstep, Apex, and MyFundedFutures.
Is there a free trial available?▾
The SMC Trade Journal App comes with a 14-day free trial. Indicators are offered at a one-time price with a satisfaction guarantee — if you're not happy within 7 days, we'll make it right.
What is the AI Confluence Score?▾
The AI Confluence Score is a proprietary weighted algorithm built into the USME that evaluates up to 10 ICT/SMC factors in real time — including OB quality, FVG presence, session timing, MTF alignment, and more — to produce a 0–100 score indicating setup strength.
