Psychological Techniques

The science behind each protocol.

Every session in The Trader's Couch is built on peer-reviewed psychological frameworks adapted for high-stakes trading environments by Brett Steenbarger's performance methodology.

01
🌬️
4-7-8 Breathing
Activates the parasympathetic nervous system within 60 seconds, reducing cortisol and lowering heart rate — measurably improving decision quality in high-stakes environments.
1.Inhale through nose for 4 counts
2.Hold breath for 7 counts
3.Exhale completely for 8 counts
4.Repeat 3–4 cycles before trading
02
🔄
Pattern Interruption
Steenbarger's core methodology for breaking emotional trading loops. The pattern interrupt physically disrupts the neural pathway between trigger (loss) and behavior (revenge trade).
1.Recognize the emotional trigger (loss)
2.Name it precisely: "This is revenge impulse"
3.Physical break — stand up, close platform
4.Audio session before returning to chart
03
🪞
Self-Monitoring
The foundational Steenbarger practice. Traders who consistently self-monitor improve rule-following by 64% within 30 days, because naming emotional states reduces their unconscious control.
1.Before open: log emotional state honestly
2.During session: notice physical signals
3.After: post-market review without judgment
4.Weekly: identify recurring patterns

"The most effective traders I have worked with don't suppress their emotions — they use them as information. When anxiety spikes before the open, that is data. The question is not how to eliminate the feeling, but how to listen to it without being controlled by it."

— Inspired by Brett N. Steenbarger · The Psychology of Trading
